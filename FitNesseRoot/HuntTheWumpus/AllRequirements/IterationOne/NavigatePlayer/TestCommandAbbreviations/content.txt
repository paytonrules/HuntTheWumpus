There are a number of abbreviations for the navigation commands.  You can see them being used below:

!include DonutMap

''Go East''
!|script|
|put|player|in cavern|1|
|enter command|Go East|
|check|cavern|2|has|Player|

''E -> Go East''
!|script|
|put|player|in cavern|1|
|enter command|E|
|check|cavern|2|has|Player|

''e -> Go East''
!|script|
|put|player|in cavern|1|
|enter command|e|
|check|cavern|2|has|Player|

''go east -> Go East''
!|script|
|put|player|in cavern|1|
|enter command|go east|
|check|cavern|2|has|Player|

''East -> Go East''
!|script|
|put|player|in cavern|1|
|enter command|East|
|check|cavern|2|has|Player|

''east -> Go East''
!|script|
|put|player|in cavern|1|
|enter command|east|
|check|cavern|2|has|Player|
